What is Case Craft?

Case Craft is a comprehensive web-based tool designed specifically for customer service representatives to efficiently generate professional case notes and SPINS (Service Provider Information Network System) entries. It streamlines the documentation process, reduces errors, and improves productivity.

Case Notes Generator Features

Customer Information

Quick input fields for contact ID, customer name, phone numbers, and account details with copy/paste functionality.

Authentication Methods

One-click selection for PIN, Passphrase, OTP, Personal Questions, Not Authorized, and verification status.

Reason Categories

Organized reason selection with categories: TV, Internet, Phone, Email, General, and Shaw ID issues.

Troubleshooting Steps

Pre-built troubleshooting steps with search functionality, drag-and-drop reordering, intelligent autocomplete, and custom step saving.

Equipment Management

Track affected equipment serial numbers, models, resolutions, and ticket numbers with tag-based input.

Callback Scheduler

Schedule callbacks with quick options (30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours, 4 hours, tomorrow, 3 days) and custom scheduling.

SPINS Generator Features

Auto-Population

Automatically imports customer information from Case Notes to SPINS for consistency and time savings.

Issue Categories

Comprehensive issue selection: Phone, Internet, Video, and General issues with detailed subcategories.

Special Instructions

Access instructions, notification preferences, and custom instructions for field technicians.

Character Counter

Real-time character counting to ensure SPINS entries meet length requirements.

Callback Tracker Features

Smart Notifications

Configurable reminder notifications with sound alerts and browser notifications before scheduled callbacks.

Advanced Filtering

Filter callbacks by status (pending/completed) and date (today, tomorrow, this week, all dates).

Statistics Dashboard

Track today's calls, pending callbacks, and completed callbacks with real-time statistics.

Callback Management

Edit, complete, or delete callbacks with full customer information and scheduling details.

Pro Tips & Best Practices

Browser Management

Use Microsoft Edge for Case Craft and Chrome for Genesis: This prevents conflicts where Genesis keeps opening in the same window. Keep Case Craft in Edge and Genesis in Chrome for better workflow management.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Search in Troubleshooting Steps: Use the search bar to quickly find specific troubleshooting steps instead of browsing through categories.

Smart Suggestion Ranking

Intelligent Autocomplete: The troubleshooting step suggestions learn from your usage patterns. Most frequently used steps appear at the top of suggestions for faster access. Access admin controls by triple-clicking the eye icon next to "Live Case Notes Preview" to enable/disable this feature.

Custom Troubleshooting Steps

Save Your Own Steps: Create and save custom troubleshooting steps that you use frequently. Click the save button (üíæ) next to any custom step to save it. Access all saved steps in the "üíæ Custom" category or through search suggestions. Use admin controls to manage, import, and export your custom steps.

Data Persistence

Auto-save Feature: All form data is automatically saved to your browser. Use the "Clear" button to reset forms when starting a new case.

Copy Functionality

Quick Copy Buttons: Use the "C" buttons next to input fields to quickly copy information to clipboard for pasting into other systems.

Notification Settings

Customize Reminders: Set your preferred reminder time (1-15 minutes) and enable sound alerts to never miss a scheduled callback.

Theme Switching

Dark/Light Mode: Switch between themes based on your preference or lighting conditions for better eye comfort during long shifts.

Smart Features & Admin Controls

Intelligent Suggestion Ranking

The system learns from your troubleshooting step usage patterns and ranks suggestions by frequency, making your most-used steps appear first.

Admin Controls

Triple-click the eye icon next to "Live Case Notes Preview" to access admin controls for managing suggestion rankings and viewing usage statistics.

Feature Toggle

Enable or disable the suggestion ranking feature through admin controls. When disabled, suggestions appear in alphabetical order.

Usage Analytics

View detailed statistics on which troubleshooting steps are used most frequently, helping identify common issues and workflow patterns.

Custom Troubleshooting Steps

Save Custom Steps

Save frequently used custom troubleshooting steps with a single click. The save button appears next to any step that isn't a default step or already saved.

Smart Integration

Custom steps automatically appear in search suggestions with a "üíæ Custom" category, making them easily accessible alongside default steps.

Custom Category

Access all your saved custom steps in the dedicated "üíæ Custom" category in the troubleshooting steps section, organized and ready to use.

Advanced Management

Manage custom steps through admin controls: edit, delete, import, and export your custom steps for backup or sharing with team members.

Import/Export

Export your custom steps as JSON files for backup or sharing. Import custom steps from other users or restore from previous backups.

Usage Tracking

Custom steps participate in the intelligent ranking system, appearing at the top of suggestions when frequently used, just like default steps.

Technical Features

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices with adaptive layouts.

Local Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser for privacy and offline access.

Theme Support

Light and dark themes with automatic preference saving.

Real-time Updates

Live preview of case notes and SPINS as you type with instant formatting.
